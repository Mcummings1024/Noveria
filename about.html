What is Noveria?

Noveria is a micronation founded by Matthew Cummings in 2016. This nation recognizes the property of New Dover to be a sovereign territory within the United States of America. Citizenship in this nation is open to anyone who wishes to join. The Goals, Organization, and Laws of Noveria are outlined in its Constitution, which can be downloaded below.
