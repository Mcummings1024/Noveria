News

  • Constitution Ratified 21 April 2016

    With his signature, the President has ratified the Constitution of the Federated States of Noveria. The Constitution, modeled after the United States Constitution, is now the law of the land. All citizens of Noveria must abide by these laws, and any amendments to the Constitution must be approved by a 3/4ths majority vote.

  • New Lands Acquired 21 April 2016

    On the same day as the signing of the Constitution, a bill was signed into law which decrees that certain islands in the Beaver Archipelago are now part of the sovereign territory of Noveria. The islands include a portion of Garden Island, and all of High and Hog Islands. The islands are uninhabited, and consist mainly of deciduous forest. An expedition to survey the newly acquired lands is expected to be approved for later this year.
